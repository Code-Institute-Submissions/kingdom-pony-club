ABOUT THE PONY CLUB The Kingdom Pony Club is the only branch located in Kerry of The Irish Pony Club which is a Voluntary Youth Organisation. It is the main equestrian organisation that provides training and competition in Showjumping, Dressage, Eventing, Mounted Games, Combined Training, Hunter Trials, Tetrathlon, other fun activities and stable management for young people. If you don't know what some of these are, it's time to look more closely!

OBJECTIVES THE IRISH PONY CLUB To encourage young people to ride and to learn to enjoy all kinds of sport connected with horses and riding. To provide instruction in riding and horsemanship, and to instil in members the proper care of their animals. To promote the highest ideals of sportsmanship, citizenship and loyalty, thereby cultivating strength of character and self-discipline.
